Cat Concentration

How To Play:

Can you find all the matching pairs of cats? Here's how to play:

  • Click two cards each turn. If they don't match, they'll turn back over and you'll have to try again!
  • If you find a matching pair of cats, the pictures will stay flipped up.
  • Try and find all matching pairs in less than 3 minutes to win!
  • Don't be afraid to try again if you don't win the first round.
